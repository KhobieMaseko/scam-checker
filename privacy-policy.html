Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — written in plain English, not legal jargon

🗓️ Last updated: April 2026
✅
The short version

We do not sell your data. We do not require an account. We store only a truncated, anonymous version of submitted messages for the public scam database. No passwords, no tracking beyond standard analytics, no third-party advertising.

1. Who we are

Am I Being Scammed? is a free AI-powered scam detection tool operated by Zenikhon Tech. The tool is available at scam-checker.netlify.app. You can contact us at zenikhon@gmail.com with any privacy-related questions.

2. What data we collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to operate the tool. Here is exactly what happens when you use the site:

Do not submit passwords, bank account numbers, Social Security or ID numbers, full credit card numbers, or any other sensitive personal information. The first 500 characters of your submission are saved to a publicly visible database.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not require you to create an account or provide your name or email address to use the tool
  • We do not store your full message — only the first 500 characters are saved
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking beyond standard analytics
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not use your submitted messages to train AI models
  • We do not collect payment information — the tool is entirely free

4. Third-party services we use

To operate this tool we use the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy.

Groq (AI inference)

Your submitted message is sent to Groq's API to be analysed by the Llama 3.3 AI model. Groq processes the message to generate a verdict and does not store it for training purposes under their standard API terms. See Groq's privacy policy.

Supabase (database)

The truncated, anonymised submission and its verdict are stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure. See Supabase's privacy policy.

Netlify (hosting)

The site is hosted on Netlify, which may collect standard server logs including IP addresses for security and performance purposes. See Netlify's privacy policy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. This collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, and approximate geographic location. It does not identify you personally. You can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. See Google's privacy policy.

ScamSearch.io (lookup feature)

If you use the Email/Phone Lookup tab, your search term is sent to the ScamSearch.io API to check their community database. See ScamSearch.io's privacy policy.

5. Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. The only cookies present are those set by Google Analytics for anonymous usage measurement, and standard session cookies set by Netlify's infrastructure for security purposes.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect your ability to use the scam checking tool.

6. The public scam database

When you submit a message for analysis, the first 500 characters along with the AI verdict, scam type, confidence score, and red flags are saved to a publicly visible database accessible via the Scam Database tab on the site.

This is intentional — it allows other users to learn from real scam examples. Before submitting, please ensure the message you paste does not contain any personally identifying information about yourself or others, such as your name, phone number, address, or financial details.

If you would like a specific submission removed from the public database, contact us at zenikhon@gmail.com and we will remove it promptly.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — you can request that we delete any data associated with you, including any database submissions you made
  • Right to correction — you can request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Right to object — you can object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at zenikhon@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, these rights are granted under GDPR and UK GDPR respectively. If you are in California, you have additional rights under CCPA.

8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we store. The database is hosted on Supabase with row-level security enabled. API keys and credentials are stored as environment variables and never exposed in client-side code. All connections to the site and its APIs are encrypted via HTTPS.

However, no system is completely secure. We recommend never submitting sensitive personal information through this tool.

9. Children's privacy

This tool is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this tool, contact us at zenikhon@gmail.com and we will remove it immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in how the tool operates or to comply with legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the tool after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
